About Me

Learn more about my journey, my passion, and what keeps me moving.

I’m currently working in retail, but over the last years I discovered how much I enjoy everything related to technology. I started learning programming on my own - first with small C++ console projects, then with Swift and SwiftUI, and later with web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Step by step, I built my first own projects, including a Workout Planner app.

What keeps me moving is the feeling of progress. Every new topic I learn, every bug I fix, and every feature I build shows me that switching into IT is the right path for me. My goal is to become a skilled developer who understands how things work under the hood and can create clean, practical solutions.

This portfolio is part of that journey. It’s my place to document what I’ve learned and to show the projects I’m working on as I move closer to my career in IT.

Workout Planner

My Workout Planner is a minimalist iOS app that lets you create custom training sessions in just a few steps. You enter a workout name, add as many exercises as you need, and assign sets and reps for each one. The interface is intentionally clean and distraction-free so you can focus on what matters.

Shopping List App

A clean and simple iOS app that helps you manage your grocery items without any clutter.
You can quickly add products, remove them once you're done, and your list stays saved thanks to SwiftData.
The app is built with SwiftUI, focuses on a smooth user experience, and keeps everything minimal so you can stay organized with zero effort.

Currency Converter App

A sleek and user-friendly iOS app built with SwiftUI that lets you convert between currencies in seconds.
Add or select currencies, enter an amount - and get instant conversion results.
Powered by modern Swift & SwiftUI, it’s designed for speed, simplicity, and day-to-day usability.
